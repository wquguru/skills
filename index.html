Why this exists

You do not need more grammar. You need better timing, tone, and phrasing.

Most engineers already know enough English to work. The problem is the last mile: standups that sound stiff, Slack messages that feel too direct, and meetings where the right sentence comes too late.

This skill focuses on the highest-frequency workplace moments where a small language upgrade creates outsized professional leverage.

What it helps with

  • Daily standup updates that sound clear and confident
  • Asking for help without sounding awkward or heavy
  • PR comments that feel collaborative instead of blunt
  • Polite pushback in meetings and design discussions
  • Light small talk that makes remote teams feel more human
